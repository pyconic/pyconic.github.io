[LOG] Securing the Future of Cars
-----------------------------------
Can we really secure these smart, connected cars? It’s going to be tough, but there are steps we can take: 
1. **Regular software updates**: Just like patching a computer, you need to keep the vehicle’s systems updated to prevent vulnerabilities.
2. **Isolate critical systems**: Keep essential systems like steering and brakes separate from non-critical functions like infotainment to reduce attack vectors.
3. **Use encryption**: Securing the communication channels between vehicle systems and external networks can prevent man-in-the-middle attacks.

As much as I admire the future of **autonomous vehicles**, I can't help but think about the risks they introduce. The complexity of these systems means new vulnerabilities could emerge at any time, leaving us vulnerable to remote control or manipulation.
